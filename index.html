Jackie Chang

Bio

I am a research assistant in Yu-Chao Wang’s Lab at National Yang Ming Chiao Tung University. In the lab, I am studying Internal Tandem Duplication in cancers by integrating and analyzing multi-omics data with statistical and machine learning approaches.

During my master’s I studied Host-Virus Interaction, specifically through the post translational modification ADP-ribosylation, of SARS-CoV-2, Chikungunya, and Influenza with Anthony Leung at the Johns Hopkins Bloomberg School of Public Health.
